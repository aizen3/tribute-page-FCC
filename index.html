Bill Gates: one of his famous sayings

  1. William Henry Gates III (born October 28, 1955) is an American business magnate, investor, author, philanthropist, humanitarian, and principal founder of Microsoft Corporation

  2. Gates is one of the best-known entrepreneurs of the personal computer revolution

  3. Gates held the positions of chairman, CEO and chief software architect, while also being the largest individual shareholder until May 2014.

  4. In 1975, Gates and Paul Allen launched Microsoft, which became the world's largest PC software company

  5. Gates led the company as chief executive officer until stepping down in January 2000, but he remained as chairman and created the position of chief software architect for himself

  6. In June 2006, Gates announced that he would be transitioning from full-time work at Microsoft to part-time work and full-time work at the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, which was established in 2000

  7. He gradually transferred his duties to Ray Ozzie and Craig Mundie.

  8. Since 1987, Gates has been included in the Forbes list of the world's wealthiest people, an index of the wealthiest documented individuals, excluding and ranking against those with wealth that is not able to be completely ascertained.

  9. From 1995 to 2017 he held the Forbes title of the richest person in the world all but four of those years, and held it consistently from March 2014 – July 2017, with an estimated net worth of US$89.9 billion as of October 2017.
